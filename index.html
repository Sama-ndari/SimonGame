The Simon Game is an electronic memory game that tests your memorization and repetition skills. The game features a sequence of colors and sounds that light up in a specific order. The player must replicate this sequence by pressing the corresponding buttons. As you progress through the game, the sequence becomes longer and more complex. The goal is to see how long you can follow the sequence without making a mistake. Good luck!
